Our Story

Range No.9 was born from a deep love for the natural world. Our fragrances are crafted to capture the essence of breathtaking landscapes and moments of pure serenity. Each scent is a personal journey, a memory bottled for you to wear.

Marina is our first scent, created to capture the crisp, salty air of a misty morning by the coast. We hope it reminds you of peaceful moments by the sea.

The Scent of Marina

A multi-layered journey for your senses, Marina unfolds with bright, fresh top notes before settling into a tranquil, woody base.

Top Notes

Fresh & Bright

The first impression of Marina is a splash of vibrant, crisp citrus. Bright grapefruit and gilled mandarin are lifted by an ethereal ozonic element, like the pure, clean air after a morning rain.

Heart Notes

Aquatic & Mineral

As the initial zest fades, the heart of the fragrance emerges. Aquatic and sea mineral elements create a watery, serene core that is both refreshing and profound, capturing the quiet depth of the ocean.

Base Notes

Warm & Earthy

The journey concludes with a grounding, warm finish. Notes of sandalwood, beach wood, and earthy vetiver anchor the scent, creating a lasting impression of the quiet comfort of the shore.
